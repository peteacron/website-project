Information Technology (I.T) in modern business is extremely important as many companies run the risk of becoming unprofitable and obsolete if they don’t keep up with emerging technologies. 1.4 Describe the risks that might impact digital projects – what causes digital projects to fail? Why?

As with any endeavor, digital projects run the risk of failure. Some examples of what can cause a digital project to fail are over ambition and not being flexible in your planning. If you don’t have an MVP (Minimum Viable Product) you run the risk of not being able to scale back your project into something financially viable if issues with deadlines and funding happen. With the quickly changing landscape of I.T and market trends being inflexible in your planning is a recipe for disaster. The need to pivot and adapt to emerging technologies is vital to being compatible and competitive when you project is completed and put into use.
